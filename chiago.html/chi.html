HOW TO BENCH PRESS PROPERLY

Steps To having A Firmer Chest


  • Make sure you do some chest warmups before attempting to bench
  • when you lie on the bench, make sure your back is straight
  • while benching, ensure to push the weights with your chest
  • carry a specific weight that you can go comfortable 10 reps with
  • have the right mindset towards the gym
  • push harder for faster results

I hope these few steps would help you to build a stronger chest and firm triceps
